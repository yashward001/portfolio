## Problem

Quant research workflows break when datasets are inconsistent, transformations are ad hoc, and experiments are not reproducible.

## Constraints

- High-volume time-series ingestion
- Consistent feature derivations across studies
- Need deterministic reruns for comparison integrity

## Approach

Developed a standardized ingestion-clean-transform pipeline with versioned datasets and reproducible experiment inputs.

## Architecture

- Scheduled ingestion jobs and validation checks
- Canonical transformation layer for shared derived features
- Experiment interface for reproducible model/evaluation runs

## Results

- Reproducible ingestion and processing pipeline for large financial time-series datasets.
- Improved confidence in cross-experiment comparability.

## What I'd Do Next

Add dataset lineage tracking and cost-aware compute scheduling.
