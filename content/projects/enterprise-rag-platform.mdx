## Problem

Enterprise teams need grounded answers from internal documents, but raw LLM calls often hallucinate and lack access control.

## Constraints

- Secure multi-user isolation
- Continuous ingestion from evolving document sources
- Need observability across retrieval and generation quality

## Approach

Implemented end-to-end ETL for ingestion, chunking, embedding, and indexing; layered retrieval evaluation and response monitoring for operational quality.

## Architecture

- ETL workers for source sync, chunking, and embedding generation
- Retrieval service with scoped access controls
- Evaluation dashboard for answer quality and latency tracking

## Results

- Multi-stage retrieval pipelines with an evaluation harness for measurable answer quality monitoring.
- Reduced hallucinations by grounding responses in indexed internal context.

## What I'd Do Next

Add hybrid retrieval (sparse + dense) and automated prompt/version regression testing.
