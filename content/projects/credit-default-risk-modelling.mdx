## Problem

Default prediction requires high recall on risky segments while preserving precision for operational decisioning.

## Constraints

- Strong class imbalance and temporal leakage risks
- Need for interpretable feature influence for risk teams

## Approach

Designed temporal aggregation features, trained gradient boosting models, and calibrated ranking thresholds for practical decision workflows.

## Architecture

- Feature engineering pipeline with leak-safe temporal windows
- LightGBM training + calibration and ranking metrics evaluation
- Importance analysis for model explainability

## Results

- Gradient-boosted ensemble with temporal feature aggregation and class-imbalance handling.
- Improved ranking quality for high-risk candidate prioritization.

## What I'd Do Next

Integrate drift monitoring and periodic recalibration for production robustness.
