## Problem

Trading strategies degrade when market behavior shifts across calm, trending, and high-volatility regimes.

## Constraints

- Non-stationary dynamics in financial series
- Need robust out-of-sample validation

## Approach

Engineered rolling-window features and trained regime classifiers aligned to downstream volatility forecasting objectives.

## Architecture

- Data prep layer for aligned returns/volatility windows
- Regime classification model with temporal validation
- Forecast evaluation module for out-of-sample performance

## Results

- Rolling-window regime classification with out-of-sample validation.
- Produced more stable downstream volatility forecasts across shift events.

## What I'd Do Next

Add hidden-state models and online adaptation for intraday regime transitions.
