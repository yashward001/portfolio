## Problem

Trend prediction in equity data is noisy and prone to overfitting, requiring disciplined validation to remain useful.

## Constraints

- Strong noise and low signal-to-noise ratio
- Need rolling validation to avoid look-ahead bias

## Approach

Trained sequence models on NASDAQ historical windows, then evaluated directional movement predictions under strict out-of-sample setups.

## Architecture

- Feature preparation pipeline for lagged and normalized series
- LSTM training loop with early-stopping and windowed validation
- Evaluation module for directional accuracy and error diagnostics

## Results

- LSTM-based directional signal analysis with rolling evaluation on held-out windows.
- Built reusable template for sequence-model market experiments.

Integrate transformer baselines and probabilistic forecasting intervals.
