## Problem

Rapid strategy research needs fast and consistent pricing + risk estimates under changing volatility and maturity assumptions.

## Constraints

- Accuracy consistency across analytical and numerical methods
- Reusable architecture for multi-strategy experiments

## Approach

Implemented Black-Scholes pricing, finite-difference checks, and Greeks (Delta, Gamma, Vega, Theta) in reusable modules with scenario sweeps.

## Architecture

- Pricing core with vectorized computations
- Risk module for Greeks and sensitivity grids
- Report layer for scenario outputs and visual diagnostics

## Results

- Analytical and numerical methods enabling rapid Greeks and scenario sweep evaluation.
- Reduced repeated notebook rework via reusable quant modules.

## What I'd Do Next

Extend to stochastic volatility models and portfolio-level stress aggregation.
